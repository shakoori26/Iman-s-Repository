About Us

Our organization has been around for 52 years. Our founders have dedicated their lives to helping those less fortunate in need of medical care. We travel to major third world countries in need of our help. We even travel within the US to areas of major poverty.

Our Mission, Vision, and Goals

The NAMD's main mission is to provide medical care to everyone, regardless of race, ethnicity, background, or financial status. We believe that everyone should be able to afford healthcare. Our main goal is to raise $3 million to support our trips to countries in need. If you wish to donate, please visit the link at the top of the page.

Our History and Past Achievements

Last summer, we hosted a trip to Somalia, a country experiencing a major food shortage. We provided medical care, and raised $1 million to help out the country. This past April, we hosted a trip to Haiti, a country experiencing a rise in gang activity. We visited the communities, dispersed food and water, provided medical attention, and raised awareness to their ongoing problems.
