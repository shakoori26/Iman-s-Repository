National Asociation of Doctors Fundraiser Dinner

Hello and welcome! All members of the National Association of Medical Doctors(NAMD) are invited to attend our prestigious fundraiser dinner. It will be held at The Plaza Hotel in NYC on July 31,2025.

About the Event

Every person is allowed 2 plus ones, and each person is charged $25 to enter. All money raised will be given to the NAMD to better our program. There will be complimentary dinner, drinks, and dessert. Our event will also feature a special performance from Grammy winning artist, Taylor Swift.

About Our Organization

We are a national association dedicated to brining people together through the miracle of medicine. We are honored to have over 200 medical doctors apart of our 50-year program. Our main goal is to provide to those in need, by providing the medical care they need, at no cost to them.
