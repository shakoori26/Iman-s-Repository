Welcome

This is the official webpage for the 2024 Women's Team USA Artistic Gymnastics Team. We are super excited to compete on the Paris stage for our country, and hope to bring home gold!

Team Highlights

We have an extremely strong team this year. With four returners, Simone Biles, Suni Lee, Jordan Chiles, and Jade Carey, we are looking better than ever. We also have rookie 16 year old, Hezly Rivera. All of these ladies are putting in the work, and we are so excited for Paris.

Simone has over 30 medals, Suni is the 2020 All-Around Gold Medalist, Jade is the 2020 floor exercise gold medalist, Jordan won silver with the team in 2020, and Hezly is fresh and ready to win!

Our biggest competitors are China, Russia, and Brazil.

Go Team USA!!
